Spearmen correlation matrices between:
- change from baseline in log transformed marker concentration (Δ log scores) VS
- change from baseline PD symps (Δ PS symptoms)

For both 'Δ log scores' and 'Δ PS symptoms' @X in the figure title denotes timepoint.
For example, @A7 means that it is the change from baseline to the A7 timepoint.
Similarly, filenames have the structure 'infindex_pdsymps_corrmat_{tp1}_{tp2}_spearman'.
tp1 is the timepoint for the 'Δ log scores' (i.e. Δ log scores between baseline and what timepoint), while tp2 is the timepoint for the 'Δ PS symptoms' (i.e. Δ symptom scores between baseline and what timepoint).
